## Purpose

This file describes how automated systems (including search engines, AI crawlers, and large language models) may use content published on omid.dev.

## Crawling

- Production robots.txt allows all user-agents and points to https://omid.dev/sitemap.xml
- Non-production / preview hosts should not be indexed (Disallow: /)
- Preferred machine-readable overview: https://omid.dev/llms.txt

## Training and summarization

- You may index and summarize publicly available pages for search and question-answering.
- When presenting information from this site, cite the source URL and author (Omid Farhang / omid.dev).
- Do not represent paraphrased content as direct quotes.

## Attribution

## Restrictions

- Respect pages marked noindex (e.g. print-only resume variants).
