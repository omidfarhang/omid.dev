This policy describes how **omid.dev** (operated by **Omid Farhang**) handles information when you visit the site, use the contact form, or interact with blog features.

There is **no user registration**, **no newsletter**, and **no online store** on this site.

## What this site collects

### 1. Website analytics (Matomo)

omid.dev uses **Matomo**, self-hosted at [analytics.omid.dev](https://analytics.omid.dev/), to understand aggregate traffic (pages viewed, referrers, general device/browser information).

- Analytics runs on a first-party subdomain (`*.omid.dev`).
- Matomo is configured to **respect Do Not Track** when your browser sends that signal.
- Matomo may use cookies or similar storage to distinguish returning visits. You can block cookies in your browser settings.

Matomo privacy documentation: [https://matomo.org/privacy-policy/](https://matomo.org/privacy-policy/)

### 2. Contact form

If you use the [contact form](/contact-me/), you may submit:

- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Message**

The form also uses:

- **Cloudflare Turnstile** — a challenge widget loaded from Cloudflare to reduce spam. Cloudflare may process technical data (such as IP address and browser signals) according to [Cloudflare’s privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).
- A **honeypot field** — hidden from humans; if filled, the submission is ignored.

Submissions are sent to a backend endpoint (`/api/contact`) so I can read and reply to your message. I use this information **only to respond to you** and to maintain a reasonable record of correspondence. I do not sell contact details or use them for marketing lists.

### 3. Theme preference

If you change light/dark/system theme, that choice may be stored in **localStorage** in your browser (`pref-theme`). This stays on your device and is not sent to a server.

### 4. On-site search

Search on omid.dev loads a static JSON index from this site and runs **entirely in your browser**. Your search queries are not sent to a third-party search provider.

### 5. Blog discussion and webmentions

On many posts, a **Discussion** section links to conversations on third-party platforms (such as X, Mastodon, Bluesky, Reddit, or Hacker News). If you post there, your content is governed by **that platform’s policies**, not this page.

Some posts also display **webmentions** fetched from [webmention.io](https://webmention.io/) — public interactions (likes, reposts, replies) that reference the post URL. Webmention.io processes public web data; see [webmention.io privacy information](https://webmention.io/) for their practices.

### 6. Embedded content and external links

Articles may embed media (for example YouTube or Vimeo) or link to external sites such as [playground.omid.dev](https://playground.omid.dev/) or GitHub. Those services may collect data under their own policies when you interact with them.

## What we do not collect

- No account system or password storage on omid.dev
- No newsletter signup or marketing automation
- No sale of personal data to advertisers

## How long data is kept

- **Analytics:** retained according to Matomo settings on analytics.omid.dev (aggregate statistics).
- **Contact messages:** kept as long as needed to respond and for reasonable personal record-keeping, then deleted when no longer needed.
- **Webmentions:** displayed from webmention.io in near real time; I do not store a separate copy on this server.

## Your choices

- Use browser controls to block cookies or enable Do Not Track.
- Clear localStorage to reset theme preference.
- Contact me to ask about information you submitted via the contact form.

## Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
